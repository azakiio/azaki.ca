CS:GO Predictor

1) Find the link of an unpcoming CS:GO match that you would like to bet on from HLTV and submit the link above

2) We will provide information about the average odds offered by bookkeepers, and our model's predicted outcome of the match.

3) We then calculate a bet recommendation based on the Kelly Criterion which produced the optimal bet for maximizing the expected bankroll growth
